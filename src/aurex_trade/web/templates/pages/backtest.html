Step 1 of 3 — What am I doing here?

This is your first sanity check. You're replaying past price data and pretending you were trading — did the strategy make money, or lose it? If it can't work on old data, it definitely won't work on new data.

What to do: Pick a strategy from the dropdown, adjust its parameters, and hit Run. The results will tell you whether this combination of settings is worth exploring further.

How the strategy works: {{ strategies[strategies.keys()|list|first].description }}

Reading the results:

  • Total P&L — did it make or lose money overall?
  • Win Rate — what % of trades were profitable?
  • Sharpe Ratio — how good is the return relative to the risk? (above 1 = decent, above 2 = strong)
  • Max Drawdown — the worst drop from a high point (how scary would it have been to hold on?)
