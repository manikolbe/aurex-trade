Test Before You Trade

Before risking real money, validate your strategy in three stages:

  • 1. Backtest

    Replay past price data through your strategy. Did it make money? This is your first sanity check.

  • 2. Parameter Sweep

    Test every combination of strategy settings and find which ones perform best.

  • 3. Walk-Forward Validation

    Prove the best settings aren't just luck by testing them on data the system has never seen.
