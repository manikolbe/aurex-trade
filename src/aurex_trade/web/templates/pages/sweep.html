Parameter Sweep

How does this work?

Parameter Sweep tries every combination of settings you provide and tells you which ones work best. Think of it like taste-testing recipes — you change one ingredient at a time and keep track of which version scores highest.

For the SMA Crossover strategy, this means testing different "lookback" lengths (how far back the averages look) and ranking the results. The winning combination appears at the top — but just because it worked on past data doesn't guarantee it works going forward. Use Walk-Forward to double-check.
